Open-source Chrome extension

Turn Moodle into NotebookLM-ready study material.

Capture Moodle course pages as you browse, export them as clean Markdown, upload the Markdown with your course syllabus to NotebookLM, and generate flashcards, summaries, and study guides faster.

How it works

  1. Open a Moodle lesson or activity page.
  2. Capture the current page in the extension.
  3. Repeat on the pages you want to save.
  4. Export the course as Markdown.
  5. Upload the Markdown and your course syllabus to NotebookLM.
  6. Ask Gemini to generate flashcards.

Why people use it

  • Reduce manual copy and paste.
  • Merge fragmented Moodle content into a single source.
  • Prepare cleaner context for NotebookLM.
  • Keep study material reusable and backed up.

Trust signals

  • Open source.

  • Backup and restore included.
  • Designed for real Moodle browsing workflows.
